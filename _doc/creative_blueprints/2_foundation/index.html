At peak mall hours guests queue at the counter, and some simply walk away. Loyalty stays invisible, first-party data goes uncaptured, and the brand holds no direct channel to bring a guest back.

问 The Problem

Convenience and loyalty live offline only. The queue caps throughput and quietly leaks revenue at the brand's busiest moments.

答 The Solution

A premium app that bridges online convenience and offline craft: order ahead, skip the queue with self-pickup, and earn through Loyal-Tang, turning an occasional treat into a repeat ritual.
