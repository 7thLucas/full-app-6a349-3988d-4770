The Foundation

Hong Tang earns the visit. The hard part is earning the next one.

The Problem

At peak mall hours customers queue at the counter — and some simply walk away. Loyalty is invisible and unrewarded, and the brand captures no first-party data and has no direct channel to bring guests back.

The Solution

A premium app that bridges online convenience and offline craftsmanship: order ahead, skip the queue with self-pickup, and earn through the Loyal-Tang rewards program — turning an occasional treat into a repeat habit.
