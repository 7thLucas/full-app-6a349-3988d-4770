Loyal-Tang · The Engine

Two currencies, two jobs

Loyal-Tang deliberately splits the spending currency from the status currency. Spending Sugar Crystals never lowers a tier; tiers track Bowls independently. All guests auto-enroll at sign-up.

◆

Sugar Crystals

Spending currency · reward points

  • Earn — 1 crystal per Rp 1.000 net spend (after discounts; excl. PB1/tax/delivery).
  • Multiplier — 1.0× / 1.25× / 1.5× / 2.0× by tier, then floored per order.
  • Spend — in the Rewards Store, producing a voucher in My Vouchers.
  • Expiry — 365 days per batch; in-app reminders within 7 days.
碗

Bowls

Status currency · tier counter

  • Count — 1 Bowl per qualifying item on a paid, completed order (2 of one = 2).
  • Window — rolling, trailing 365 days; older Bowls age out.
  • Sets tier only — never spent, never affected by redemptions.
  • Excludes — free / Rp 0 / trial / special-discount items & 3rd-party orders.
例

Worked example — Grassjelly Signature Rp 50.000 + Royal Milk Tea Rp 19.000 = Rp 69.000; voucher −Rp 10.000 → net Rp 59.000. At Seeker (1.0×) → 59 crystals; at Pioneer (1.5×) → 88. Both add +2 Bowls.
