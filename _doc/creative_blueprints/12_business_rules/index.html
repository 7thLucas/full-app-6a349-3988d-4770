Business Rules & Logic

The rules that keep money and trust correct

Resolved decisions, no open questions at this revision. Order placement is idempotent; no double-charge.

01

Vouchers

No stacking. Exactly one voucher OR one promo code per order; a new one replaces the current. Validated at checkout against cart + fulfillment mode.

02

Order Lock

No edits after payment: items, customization, store, mode all locked. To change, cancel within the window (refund to original method), then reorder.

03

Payments & Refunds

QRIS, e-wallets (GoPay/OVO/DANA/ShopeePay/LinkAja), Visa/Mastercard. Refunds to original method, up to 15 working days. No card data on-device.

04

Cancellation

“Cancelled” = system auto-cancel + refund. If an extended peak-hour wait was shown and the guest knowingly proceeded, wait-time cancellation does not apply.

05

Earning Exclusions

No crystals or Bowls on free, Rp 0, trial, special-discount items, or any order via GoFood / GrabFood / ShopeeFood. Walk-in: member QR must be scanned at the till.

06

Referrals

Unique code, optional, first-time registrants only. Referrer earns once the referee completes a first qualifying purchase; referee keeps welcome vouchers. Anti-abuse caps per campaign.
