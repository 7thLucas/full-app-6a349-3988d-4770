Business Rules & Logic

The rules that keep money and trust correct

Resolved decisions, no open questions at this revision. Idempotent order placement; no double-charge.

Vouchers

No stacking — exactly one voucher OR one promo code per order. Applying a new one replaces the current. Validated at checkout against cart + fulfillment mode.

Order Lock

No edits after payment — items, customization, store, mode all locked. To change: cancel within the cancellation window (refund to original method), then reorder.

Payments & Refunds

QRIS, e-wallets (GoPay/OVO/DANA/ShopeePay/LinkAja), Visa/Mastercard. Refunds to original method, up to 15 working days. No card data on-device.

Cancellation

"Cancelled" = system auto-cancel + refund. If extended peak-hour wait was shown and the user knowingly proceeded, wait-time cancellation doesn't apply.

Earning Exclusions

No crystals/Bowls on free, Rp 0, trial/tasting, special-discount items — or any order via GoFood / GrabFood / ShopeeFood. Walk-in: member QR must be scanned at the till; no retroactive points.

Referrals

Unique code, optional, first-time registrants only (copy-paste recommended). Referrer earns once referee completes first qualifying purchase; referee keeps welcome vouchers. Anti-abuse caps per campaign.
